Upstream / Downstream

As you travel east in North Carolina, you pass through metropolitan and then agricultural counties before reaching Albemarle-Pamlico Estuary System, the second largest in the country behind the Chesapeake Bay. That’s where the runoff from both cities and farms ends up, which has major ecological and economic consequences for the coast. Most North Carolinians don’t realize the impact they have downstream. Upstream / Downstream will help you exploring the connections between urban, rural and coastal North Carolina through its waterways.
